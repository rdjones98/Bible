Psalm 148 & New Testament Fulfillment

Psalm 148 (Masoretic Text – Modern Translation) New Testament Reflection / Fulfillment
1. Praise the LORD (YHWH – He is)! Praise the LORD (YHWH – He is) from the heavens; praise Him in the heights! Luke 2:13-14 – "And suddenly there was with the angel a multitude of the heavenly host praising God and saying, 'Glory to God in the highest, and on earth peace among those with whom He is pleased!'"
2. Praise Him, all His messengers! Praise Him, all His hosts! Hebrews 1:6 – "Let all God’s angels worship Him."
3. Praise Him, sun and moon! Praise Him, all you shining stars! Revelation 21:23 – "And the city has no need of sun or moon to shine on it, for the glory of God gives it light, and its lamp is the Lamb."
4. Praise Him, highest heavens, and you waters above the heavens! Revelation 5:13 – "And I heard every creature in heaven and on earth and under the earth and in the sea, and all that is in them, saying, 'To Him who sits on the throne and to the Lamb be blessing and honor and glory and might forever and ever!'"
5. Let them praise the name of the LORD (YHWH – He is), for He commanded, and they were created. John 1:3 – "All things were made through Him, and without Him was not any thing made that was made."
6. He established them forever and ever; He gave a decree, and it shall not pass away. Matthew 24:35 – "Heaven and earth will pass away, but my words will not pass away."
7. Praise the LORD (YHWH – He is) from the earth, sea monsters and all the depths, Philippians 2:10 – "...so that at the name of Jesus every knee should bow, in heaven and on earth and under the earth..."
8. Fire and hail, snow and mist, stormy wind fulfilling His word! Mark 4:41 – "And they were filled with great fear and said to one another, 'Who then is this, that even the wind and the sea obey Him?'"
9. Mountains and all hills, fruit trees and all cedars! Romans 8:19 – "For the creation waits with eager longing for the revealing of the sons of God."
10. Wild animals and all cattle, creeping things and flying birds! Colossians 1:16 – "For by Him all things were created, in heaven and on earth, visible and invisible..."
11. Kings of the earth and all peoples, princes and all judges of the earth! Revelation 1:5 – "...Jesus Christ the faithful witness, the firstborn of the dead, and the ruler of kings on earth."
12. Young men and also young women, old men with children! Acts 2:17 – "'Your sons and your daughters shall prophesy, and your young men shall see visions, and your old men shall dream dreams...'"
13. Let them praise the name of the LORD (YHWH – He is), for His name alone is exalted; His majesty is above earth and heaven. Philippians 2:9 – "Therefore God has highly exalted Him and bestowed on Him the name that is above every name..."
14. And He has raised up a horn for His people, praise for all His faithful ones, for the children of Israel (Yisra’el – God strives), the people near to Him. Praise the LORD (YHWH – He is)! Luke 1:69 – "And He has raised up a horn of salvation for us in the house of His servant David..."
