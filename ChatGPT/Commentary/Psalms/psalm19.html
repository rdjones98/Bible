Psalm 19 and New Testament Fulfillment

Psalm 19 (Masoretic Text - Modern English) New Testament Reflection or Fulfillment
1 The heavens declare the glory of God (El – Mighty One)... Romans 1:19–20 – "What can be known about God is plain... His invisible attributes... have been clearly perceived in the things that have been made."
4 Their line has gone out through all the earth... Romans 10:17–18 – Paul quotes this verse to describe the global reach of the gospel: "Their voice has gone out to all the earth..."
5 Like a bridegroom leaving his chamber... John 3:29 – "The one who has the bride is the bridegroom..."
Revelation 19:7 – "The marriage of the Lamb has come..."
7–9 The Torah, testimony, precepts, commandment, fear, and judgments of the LORD... Matthew 5:17 – "I did not come to abolish the Law but to fulfill it."
2 Timothy 3:16 – "All Scripture is breathed out by God..."
10 More desirable than gold... sweeter than honey... Matthew 13:44 – "The kingdom of heaven is like treasure hidden in a field..."
1 Peter 2:2–3 – "...long for the pure spiritual milk... if indeed you have tasted that the Lord is good."
11 By them Your servant is warned... James 1:22–25 – "Be doers of the word... the one who looks into the perfect law... will be blessed in his doing."
12 Who can discern his errors? Cleanse me from hidden faults. 1 John 1:9 – "If we confess our sins, He is faithful and just to forgive... and to cleanse us..."
13 Keep Your servant from arrogant sins... Romans 6:14 – "Sin will have no dominion over you..."
Galatians 5:16 – "Walk by the Spirit, and you will not gratify the desires of the flesh."
14 Let the words of my mouth... be acceptable... Hebrews 13:15 – "Let us continually offer up a sacrifice of praise... the fruit of lips that acknowledge His name."
Romans 12:1 – "Present your bodies as a living sacrifice... acceptable to God..."
