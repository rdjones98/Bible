Psalm 2 and New Testament Fulfillment

Psalm 2 (Masoretic Text - Modern English) New Testament Reflection or Fulfillment
1 Why do the nations rage, and the peoples plot in vain? Acts 4:25–26 – The early church quotes this psalm, acknowledging the hostile plotting against Jesus.
2 The kings of the earth set themselves, and the rulers take counsel together, against the LORD (YHWH – He is) and against His Anointed... Hebrews 1:5 – Quoting Psalm 2:7, affirming Jesus as God’s Son.
Acts 13:32–33 – Paul declares Jesus as the Son begotten by God, fulfilling this psalm.
4 He who sits in the heavens laughs; the Lord (Adonai – Master) holds them in derision. Matthew 28:18 – Jesus proclaims all authority in heaven and on earth.
Revelation 19:11–16 – Jesus as the King who judges and reigns with power.
6 “But I have set My king on Tziyon (Zion – Fortress), My holy mountain.” Luke 1:32–33 – The angel announces Jesus will reign on the throne of David forever.
7 I will proclaim the decree: The LORD (YHWH – He is) said to me, “You are My Son; today I have begotten You.” Hebrews 5:5 – Jesus is declared the Son of God.
Romans 1:3–4 – Jesus is declared Son of God by resurrection.
8 Ask of Me, and I will make the nations Your inheritance, and the ends of the earth Your possession. Matthew 28:18–20 – Jesus commands the disciples to make disciples of all nations.
Revelation 11:15 – The kingdom of the world becomes the kingdom of our Lord and His Christ.
9 You shall break them with a rod of iron; You shall shatter them like a potter’s vessel.” Revelation 2:27 – Jesus will rule the nations with a rod of iron.
Revelation 19:15 – Jesus wields a sharp sword and rules with a rod of iron.
11 Serve the LORD (YHWH – He is) with fear, and rejoice with trembling. Hebrews 12:28–29 – We must worship God with reverence and awe.
12 Kiss the Son, lest He be angry and you perish in the way, for His wrath may soon be kindled. Blessed are all who take refuge in Him. John 3:36 – Whoever believes in the Son has eternal life; whoever rejects Him will not see life.
Romans 10:9 – Confess Jesus as Lord and believe in His resurrection to be saved.
