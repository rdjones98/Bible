Psalm 150 & New Testament Fulfillment

Psalm 150 (Masoretic Text – Modern Translation) New Testament Reflection / Fulfillment
1. Praise the LORD (YHWH – He is)! Praise God (El – Mighty One) in His sanctuary; praise Him in the firmament of His power! Revelation 15:2-3 – "And I saw what appeared to be a sea of glass mingled with fire—and also those who had conquered the beast... were singing the song of Moses... and the song of the Lamb..."
2. Praise Him for His mighty deeds; praise Him according to His abundant greatness! Luke 1:49 – "For He who is mighty has done great things for me, and holy is His name."
3. Praise Him with the blast of the ram’s horn; praise Him with harp and lyre! Revelation 14:2 – "And I heard a voice from heaven like the sound of many waters and like the sound of loud thunder. The voice I heard was like the sound of harpists playing on their harps."
4. Praise Him with tambourine and dance; praise Him with strings and pipe! Luke 15:25 – "Now his older son was in the field, and as he came and drew near to the house, he heard music and dancing."
5. Praise Him with sounding cymbals; praise Him with resounding cymbals! 1 Corinthians 13:1 – "If I speak in the tongues of men and of angels, but have not love, I am a noisy gong or a clanging cymbal."
6. Let everything that has breath praise the LORD (YHWH – He is)! Praise the LORD (YHWH – He is)! Revelation 5:13 – "And I heard every creature in heaven and on earth and under the earth and in the sea... saying, 'To Him who sits on the throne and to the Lamb be blessing and honor and glory and might forever and ever!'"
