Psalm 24 and New Testament Fulfillment

Psalm 24 (Masoretic Text - Modern English) New Testament Reflection or Fulfillment
1 The earth is the LORD’s (YHWH – He is), and everything in it... Colossians 1:16 – "For by Him all things were created... all things were created through Him and for Him."
3 Who may ascend the mountain of the LORD (YHWH – He is)? Hebrews 12:22–24 – "But you have come to Mount Zion... to Jesus the mediator of a new covenant..."
4 The one who has clean hands and a pure heart... Matthew 5:8 – "Blessed are the pure in heart, for they shall see God."
5 He will receive blessing from the LORD (YHWH – He is)... Ephesians 1:3 – "Blessed be the God... who has blessed us in Christ with every spiritual blessing..."
6 Such is the generation of those who seek Him... John 4:23 – "The hour is coming... when the true worshipers will worship the Father in spirit and truth..."
7 Lift up your heads, O gates!... Acts 1:9–11 – Jesus ascends into heaven.
Revelation 3:20 – "Behold, I stand at the door and knock..."
Psalm 24 is traditionally associated with Jesus' triumphal ascension.
8 Who is this King of glory? The LORD (YHWH – He is), strong and mighty... Revelation 19:16 – "King of kings and Lord of lords."
1 Corinthians 2:8 – "...they would not have crucified the Lord of glory."
10 The LORD (YHWH – He is) of hosts... He is the King of glory. James 2:1 – "Our glorious Lord Jesus Christ."
Philippians 2:9–11 – "God exalted Him... every knee should bow..."
