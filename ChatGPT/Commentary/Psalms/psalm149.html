Psalm 149 & New Testament Fulfillment

Psalm 149 (Masoretic Text – Modern Translation) New Testament Reflection / Fulfillment
1. Praise the LORD (YHWH – He is)! Sing to the LORD (YHWH – He is) a new song, His praise in the assembly of the faithful! Revelation 5:9 – "And they sang a new song, saying, 'Worthy are You to take the scroll and to open its seals...'"
2. Let Israel (Yisra’el – God strives) rejoice in his Maker; let the children of Zion (Tziyon – Parched Place) be joyful in their King! Matthew 21:5 – "'Say to the daughter of Zion, Behold, your King is coming to you, humble and mounted on a donkey...'"
3. Let them praise His name with dancing; let them make music to Him with tambourine and lyre! Luke 15:25 – "Now his older son was in the field, and as he came and drew near to the house, he heard music and dancing."
4. For the LORD (YHWH – He is) takes pleasure in His people; He adorns the humble with salvation. James 4:6 – "God opposes the proud but gives grace to the humble."
5. Let the faithful exult in glory; let them shout for joy on their beds. 1 Peter 1:8 – "...you rejoice with joy that is inexpressible and filled with glory..."
6. The high praises of God (Elohim – Mighty Ones) in their throats and a two-edged sword in their hands, Hebrews 4:12 – "For the word of God is living and active, sharper than any two-edged sword..."
7. to execute vengeance on the nations and punishments on the peoples, Revelation 19:15 – "From His mouth comes a sharp sword with which to strike down the nations..."
8. to bind their kings with chains and their nobles with iron shackles, 2 Corinthians 10:4 – "For the weapons of our warfare are not of the flesh but have divine power to destroy strongholds."
9. to carry out the written judgment against them. This is the honor of all His faithful ones. Praise the LORD (YHWH – He is)! Jude 1:14-15 – "Behold, the Lord comes with ten thousands of His holy ones, to execute judgment on all..."
