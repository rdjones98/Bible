Psalm 1 and New Testament Fulfillment

Psalm 1 (Masoretic Text - Modern English) New Testament Reflection or Fulfillment
1 Blessed is the man who does not walk in the counsel of the wicked... Matthew 5:1–12 – The Beatitudes begin with "Blessed are..." and describe the path of righteousness in contrast to the world.
Romans 12:2 – "Do not conform to the pattern of this world..."
2 But his delight is in the Torah of the LORD (YHWH – He is)... Matthew 5:17–19 – Jesus affirms the Torah and its fulfillment.
John 15:10 – "If you keep My commandments, you will abide in My love..."
3 He will be like a tree planted by streams of water... John 7:38 – "Whoever believes in Me... rivers of living water will flow from within them."
Revelation 22:1–2 – The tree of life beside the river, yielding fruit monthly.
4 The wicked are not so, but are like chaff that the wind drives away. Matthew 3:12 – "He will burn up the chaff with unquenchable fire."
Luke 8:6 – The parable of the sower and seed on poor soil (unfruitful hearts).
5 Therefore the wicked will not stand in the judgment... John 5:29 – "...those who have done evil will rise to be condemned."
2 Corinthians 5:10 – "We must all appear before the judgment seat of Christ..."
6 For the LORD (YHWH – He is) knows the way of the righteous... John 10:14 – "I am the good shepherd; I know My own and My own know Me."
2 Timothy 2:19 – "The Lord knows those who are His."
