Psalm 139 and Christ: Side-by-Side Comparison

Psalm 139 New Testament Fulfillment in Christ
Psalm 139:1–2
“O LORD (YHWH – He is), You have searched me and known me. You know when I sit down and when I rise up; You understand my thoughts from afar.”		 Hebrews 4:13
“Nothing in all creation is hidden from God’s sight. Everything is uncovered and laid bare before the eyes of Him to whom we must give account.”
Psalm 139:7–10
“Where can I go from Your Spirit? Or where can I flee from Your presence? If I ascend to the heavens, You are there; if I make my bed in Sheol, behold, You are there. If I take the wings of the dawn and dwell in the uttermost parts of the sea, even there Your hand will lead me, and Your right hand will hold me fast.”		 Matthew 28:20
“And behold, I am with you always, to the end of the age.”
John 1:14
“The Word became flesh and dwelt among us...” (Christ’s omnipresence through His incarnation)
Psalm 139:13–16
“For You formed my inward parts; You wove me together in my mother’s womb. I praise You, for I am fearfully and wonderfully made... Your eyes saw my unformed substance; in Your book were written all the days that were formed for me.”		 John 1:3
“Through Him all things were made; without Him nothing was made that has been made.”
Colossians 1:16
“For by Him all things were created: things in heaven and on earth...” (Christ as Creator and sustainer of life)
Psalm 139:17–18
“How precious to me are Your thoughts, O God (Elohim – Mighty Ones)! How vast is the sum of them! If I should count them, they would outnumber the sand.”		 Romans 11:33
“Oh, the depth of the riches and wisdom and knowledge of God! How unsearchable are His judgments and His ways!”
Psalm 139:19–22
“O that You would slay the wicked, O God (Elohim – Mighty Ones)! Do I not hate those who hate You, O LORD? I hate them with complete hatred.”		 John 3:19
“This is the verdict: Light has come into the world, but people loved darkness instead of light because their deeds were evil.”
Romans 12:9
“Hate what is evil; cling to what is good.”
Psalm 139:23–24
“Search me, O God (Elohim – Mighty Ones), and know my heart; test me and know my anxious thoughts. See if there is any offensive way in me, and lead me in the everlasting way.”		 John 14:6
“I am the way, and the truth, and the life. No one comes to the Father except through Me.”
Hebrews 4:12
“For the word of God is living and active, sharper than any two-edged sword...”
