Psalm 18 and New Testament Fulfillment in Jesus

Psalm 18 (Masoretic Text Translation) New Testament Fulfillment in Jesus
1–2: I love You, O LORD (YHWH – He is), my strength. The LORD is my rock, my fortress, and my deliverer... in whom I take refuge. Matthew 3:17 – "This is my beloved Son, with whom I am well pleased."
Luke 4:18 – "He has sent me to proclaim liberty to the captives..."
3: I call upon the LORD, who is worthy of praise, and I am saved from my enemies. Romans 10:13 – "Everyone who calls on the name of the Lord will be saved."
John 16:33 – "Take heart; I have overcome the world."
4–6: The cords of death encompassed me... In my distress I called upon the LORD... He heard my voice. Hebrews 5:7 – "Jesus offered up prayers... to Him who was able to save Him from death."
Luke 22:44 – "Being in agony, He prayed more earnestly..."
7–15: Then the earth shook and trembled... He bowed the heavens and came down... The LORD thundered from heaven. Matthew 27:51 – "The earth shook, and the rocks were split."
Revelation 19:11–16 – Christ returns in power and judgment.
16–19: He sent from on high, He took me... He rescued me because He delighted in me. John 17:24 – "You loved me before the foundation of the world."
Colossians 1:13 – "He has delivered us from the domain of darkness."
20–24: The LORD dealt with me according to my righteousness... I have kept His ways. 1 Peter 2:22 – "He committed no sin, neither was deceit found in His mouth."
Hebrews 4:15 – "Yet without sin."
25–29: With the merciful You show Yourself merciful... For by You I can run against a troop... You light my lamp. Luke 6:36 – "Be merciful, even as your Father is merciful."
John 8:12 – "I am the light of the world."
30–36: His way is perfect... He trains my hands for war... Your right hand supports me. 2 Corinthians 10:4 – "The weapons of our warfare are not of the flesh..."
Acts 2:33 – "Exalted at the right hand of God..."
37–42: I pursued my enemies and overtook them... I crushed them so they could not rise. Colossians 2:15 – "He disarmed the rulers and authorities and put them to open shame, triumphing over them."
43–45: You delivered me from strife... You made me head of the nations... foreigners come trembling. Philippians 2:10–11 – "Every knee should bow... and every tongue confess that Jesus Christ is Lord."
Revelation 15:4 – "All nations will come and worship before you."
46–50: The LORD lives, and blessed be my rock... Great salvation He brings to His king and shows steadfast love to His anointed. Luke 1:69–71 – "He has raised up a horn of salvation for us... to save us from our enemies."
Acts 13:33 – "You are my Son, today I have begotten you."
