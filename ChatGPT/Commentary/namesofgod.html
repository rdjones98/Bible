Hebrew Names of God

  • אֶהְיֶה אֲשֶׁר אֶהְיֶה (Ehyeh Asher Ehyeh): I AM WHO I AM (Exodus 3:14)
  • יהוה (YHWH) – "Yahweh": He is (3rd person form of I AM)
  • אָנִי יְהוָה (Ani YHWH) – "I am the Lord": I am the Lord
  • אֱלֹהִים (Elohim) – "God": Mighty One, Supreme God
  • אֵל (El) – "God": Strong One
  • אֵל שַׁדַּי (El Shaddai) – "God Almighty": Genesis 17:1
  • אֵל עֶלְיוֹן (El Elyon) – "God Most High": Genesis 14:18
  • אֵל רֳאִי (El Roi) – "The God Who Sees Me": Genesis 16:13
  • אֵל קַנָּא (El Qanna) – "Jealous God": Exodus 34:14
  • אֲדֹנָי (Adonai) – "Lord": Master, Sovereign
  • יְהוָה צְבָאוֹת (YHWH Tzeva’ot) – "The Lord of Hosts": Commander of Armies
  • יְהוָה יִרְאֶה (YHWH Yireh) – "The Lord Will Provide": Genesis 22:14
  • יְהוָה רֹפְאֶךָ (YHWH Ropheka) – "The Lord Who Heals": Exodus 15:26
  • יְהוָה שָׁלוֹם (YHWH Shalom) – "The Lord is Peace": Judges 6:24
  • יְהוָה רֹעִי (YHWH Ro’i) – "The Lord is My Shepherd": Psalm 23:1
  • יְהוָה צִדְקֵנוּ (YHWH Tzidkenu) – "The Lord Our Righteousness": Jeremiah 23:6
  • יְהוָה נִסִּי (YHWH Nissi) – "The Lord is My Banner": Exodus 17:15
  • יְהוָה מְקַדִּשְׁכֶם (YHWH Mekaddishkem) – "The Lord Who Sanctifies You": Leviticus 20:8
  • אֵל גִּבּוֹר (El Gibbor) – "Mighty God": Isaiah 9:6
  • אֱלוֹהַּ (Eloah) – "God": Used in poetic and wisdom literature like Job
  • אֱלוֹהֵי יִשְׂרָאֵל (Elohei Yisrael) – "God of Israel": Genesis 33:20
