Psalm 51 and New Testament Fulfillment

Psalm 51 (Masoretic Text - Modern English) New Testament Reflection or Fulfillment
1 Have mercy on me, O God (Elohim – Mighty Ones), according to Your steadfast love... Titus 3:5 – "He saved us, not because of righteous things we had done, but because of His mercy..."
2 Wash me thoroughly from my guilt, and cleanse me from my sin. 1 John 1:7 – "The blood of Jesus… cleanses us from all sin."
10 Create in me a pure heart, O God (Elohim – Mighty Ones)... 2 Corinthians 5:17 – "If anyone is in Christ, he is a new creation..."
Ezekiel 36:26 (quoted in NT theology) – "I will give you a new heart..."
11 Do not cast me away from Your presence, and do not take Your Holy Spirit from me. John 14:16–17 – "I will ask the Father, and He will give you another Helper... the Spirit of truth."
Acts 2:4 – The Holy Spirit given at Pentecost.
12 Restore to me the joy of Your salvation... Romans 5:11 – "We rejoice in God through our Lord Jesus Christ, through whom we have now received reconciliation."
14 Deliver me from the guilt of bloodshed, O God (Elohim – Mighty Ones)... Hebrews 9:14 – "How much more will the blood of Christ… cleanse our consciences from dead works..."
15 O Lord (Adonai – My Lord), open my lips, and my mouth will declare Your praise. Hebrews 13:15 – "Let us continually offer to God a sacrifice of praise—the fruit of lips that openly profess His name."
17 The sacrifices of God are a broken spirit... Matthew 5:3–4 – "Blessed are the poor in spirit... those who mourn..."
Luke 18:13–14 – The tax collector’s contrite prayer leads to justification.
