Exodus 37 - Literal Translation

1. And Bezaleel (Betzalel - in the shadow of God) made the ark (Aron - chest) of shittim wood: two cubits and a half was the length of it, and a cubit and a half the breadth of it, and a cubit and a half the height of it:

2. And he overlaid it with pure gold within and without, and made a crown of gold to it round about.

3. And he cast for it four rings of gold, to be set by the four corners of it; even two rings upon the one side of it, and two rings upon that side of it.

4. And he made staves (poles) of shittim wood, and overlaid them with gold.

5. And he put the staves (poles) into the rings by the sides of the ark (Aron - chest), to bear the ark (Aron - chest).

6. And he made the mercy seat (Kapporeth - lid) of pure gold: two cubits and a half was the length thereof, and a cubit and a half the breadth thereof.

7. And he made two cherubims (Keruvim - angels) of gold, beaten out of one piece made he them, on the two ends of the mercy seat (Kapporeth - lid);

8. One cherub (Keruv - angel) on the one end, and another cherub (Keruv - angel) on that end: out of the mercy seat (Kapporeth - lid) made he the cherubims (Keruvim - angels) on the two ends thereof.

9. And the cherubims (Keruvim - angels) spread out their wings on high, and covered with their wings over the mercy seat (Kapporeth - lid), with their faces one to another; even to the mercy seat (Kapporeth - lid)ward were the faces of the cherubims (Keruvim - angels).

10. And he made the table (Shulchan - table) of shittim wood: two cubits was the length thereof, and a cubit the breadth thereof, and a cubit and a half the height thereof:

11. And he overlaid it with pure gold, and made thereunto a crown of gold round about.

12. And he made unto it a border of a handbreadth round about, and made a crown of gold for the border thereof round about.

13. And he cast for it four rings of gold, and put the rings upon the four corners that were in the four feet thereof.

14. Over against the border were the rings, the places for the staves (poles) to bear the table (Shulchan - table).

15. And he made the staves (poles) of shittim wood, and overlaid them with gold, to bear the table (Shulchan - table).

16. And he made the vessels which were upon the table (Shulchan - table), his dishes, and his spoons, and his covers, and his bowls, to cover withal, of pure gold.

17. And he made the candlestick (Menorah - lampstand) of pure gold: of beaten work made he the candlestick (Menorah - lampstand); his shaft, and his branch, his bowls, his knops (buds), and his flowers, were of the same:

18. And six branches going out of the sides thereof; three branches of the candlestick (Menorah - lampstand) out of the one side of it, and three branches of the candlestick (Menorah - lampstand) out of that side of it:

19. Three bowls made after the fashion of almonds (Shaked - almond), with a knop (bud) and a flower in one branch; and three bowls made like almonds (Shaked - almond), with a knop (bud) and a flower in another branch: so throughout the six branches going out of the candlestick (Menorah - lampstand).

20. And in the candlestick (Menorah - lampstand) were four bowls made like almonds (Shaked - almond), with their knops (buds) and their flowers:

21. And a knop (bud) under two branches of the same, and a knop (bud) under two branches of the same, and a knop (bud) under two branches of the same, according to the six branches going out of it.

22. Their knops (buds) and their branches were of the same: all of it was one beaten work of pure gold.

23. And he made his seven lamps, and his snuffers, and his snuff dishes, of pure gold.

24. Of a talent of pure gold made he it, and all the vessels thereof.

25. And he made the altar (Mizbeach - altar) of incense (Ketoret - incense) of shittim wood: the length of it was a cubit, and the breadth of it a cubit; it was four square, and two cubits was the height of it: the horns thereof were of the same.

26. And he overlaid it with pure gold, the top thereof, and the sides thereof round about, and the horns thereof; also he made unto it a crown of gold round about.

27. And he made two rings of gold for it under the crown thereof, by the two corners of it, upon the two sides thereof, to be places for the staves (poles) to bear it withal.

28. And he made the staves (poles) of shittim wood, and overlaid them with gold.

29. And he made the holy anointing oil (Shemen HaMishchah - anointing oil) and the pure incense (Ketoret - incense) of sweet spices, according to the work of the apothecary.
