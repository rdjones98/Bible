Exodus 26

1. Moreover, you shall make the tabernacle with ten curtains (yeri'ot - "curtains") of fine twined linen and blue and purple and scarlet, with cherubim skillfully worked (ma'aseh - "work"), shall you make them.

2. The length (orekh - "length") of each curtain (yeri'ah - "curtain") shall be twenty-eight cubits, and the breadth (reva'ah - "breadth") of each curtain (yeri'ah - "curtain") four cubits; all the curtains (yeri'ot - "curtains") shall have one measure (mishkal - "measure").

3. Five curtains (yeri'ot - "curtains") shall be coupled together (chavur - "joined"), one to another; and five other curtains (yeri'ot - "curtains") shall be coupled together (chavur - "joined") one to another.

4. And you shall make loops (luchos - "loops") of blue on the edge (kaneh - "edge") of the one curtain (yeri'ah - "curtain") from the selvedge (petaá¸¥ - "edge") in the coupling (chibur - "joining"); and likewise shall you make in the edge (kaneh - "edge") of the outermost curtain (yeri'ah - "curtain") in the second coupling (chibur - "joining").

5. Fifty loops (luchos - "loops") shall you make in the one curtain (yeri'ah - "curtain") and fifty loops (luchos - "loops") shall you make in the edge (kaneh - "edge") of the curtain (yeri'ah - "curtain") that is in the second coupling (chibur - "joining"); the loops (luchos - "loops") shall be opposite one another.

6. And you shall make fifty clasps (chashuq - "clasps") of gold, and couple (chavarta - "join") the curtains (yeri'ot - "curtains") together with the clasps (chashuq - "clasps"), and it shall be one tabernacle.

7. And you shall make curtains (yeri'ot - "curtains") of goats' hair to be a covering (kiseh - "covering") upon the tabernacle; eleven curtains (yeri'ot - "curtains") shall you make them.

8. The length (orekh - "length") of one curtain (yeri'ah - "curtain") shall be thirty cubits, and the breadth (reva'ah - "breadth") of one curtain (yeri'ah - "curtain") four cubits; the eleven curtains (yeri'ot - "curtains") shall have one measure (mishkal - "measure").

9. And you shall couple five curtains (yeri'ot - "curtains") by themselves, and six curtains (yeri'ot - "curtains") by themselves, and you shall double over the sixth curtain (yeri'ah - "curtain") at the forefront of the tabernacle.

10. And you shall make fifty loops (luchos - "loops") on the edge (kaneh - "edge") of the one curtain (yeri'ah - "curtain") that is outermost in the coupling (chibur - "joining"), and fifty loops (luchos - "loops") in the edge (kaneh - "edge") of the curtain (yeri'ah - "curtain") that couples the second.

11. And you shall make fifty clasps (chashuq - "clasps") of bronze, and put the clasps (chashuq - "clasps") into the loops (luchos - "loops"), and couple the tent together, that it may be one.

12. And the remnant (yether - "remnant") that remains of the curtains (yeri'ot - "curtains") of the tent (ohel - "tent"), the half curtain (yeri'ah - "curtain") that remains, shall hang over the back (achor - "back") of the tabernacle.

13. And a cubit on this side and a cubit on that side of that which remains of the length (orekh - "length") of the curtains (yeri'ot - "curtains") of the tent (ohel - "tent"), it shall hang over the sides (tsal - "side") of the tabernacle, on this side and on that side, to cover it.

14. And you shall make a covering (kiseh - "covering") for the tent (ohel - "tent") of ram skins dyed red (adom - "red") and a covering (kiseh - "covering") above of badger skins (tahash - "badger skins").

15. And you shall make boards (kerashim - "boards") for the tabernacle of acacia wood standing up.

16. Ten cubits shall be the length (orekh - "length") of a board (keresh - "board"), and a cubit and a half shall be the breadth (reva'ah - "breadth") of a board (keresh - "board").

17. Two tenons (yad - "tenons") shall there be in each board (keresh - "board"), fastened one to another; thus shall you make for all the boards (kerashim - "boards") of the tabernacle.

18. And you shall make the boards (kerashim - "boards") for the tabernacle twenty boards (kerashim - "boards") for the south side (negba - "south") southward.

19. And you shall make forty sockets (adanim - "sockets") of silver under the twenty boards (kerashim - "boards"); two sockets (adanim - "sockets") under one board (keresh - "board") for its two tenons (yad - "tenons"), and two sockets (adanim - "sockets") under another board (keresh - "board") for its two tenons (yad - "tenons").

20. And for the second side of the tabernacle on the north side (tsafon - "north") there shall be twenty boards (kerashim - "boards"),

21. And their forty sockets (adanim - "sockets") of silver; two sockets (adanim - "sockets") under one board (keresh - "board") and two sockets (adanim - "sockets") under another board (keresh - "board").

22. And for the sides of the tabernacle westward (yam - "west") you shall make six boards (kerashim - "boards").

23. And two boards (kerashim - "boards") shall you make for the corners (peneh - "corner") of the tabernacle (mishkan - "tabernacle) in the two sides.

24. And they shall be coupled (chavur - "joined") together beneath, and they shall be coupled (chavur - "joined") together at the top of it, to one ring (tabat - "ring"); thus shall it be for both of them, for the two corners (peneh - "corner").

25. And they shall be eight boards (kerashim - "boards"), and their sockets (adanim - "sockets") of silver, sixteen sockets (adanim - "sockets"); two sockets (adanim - "sockets") under one board (keresh - "board"), and two sockets (adanim - "sockets") under another board (keresh - "board").

26. And you shall make bars (bar - "bars") of acacia wood; five for the boards (kerashim - "boards") of the one side (tsal - "side") of the tabernacle,

27. And five bars (bar - "bars") for the boards (kerashim - "boards") of the other side (tsal - "side") of the tabernacle, and five bars (bar - "bars") for the boards (kerashim - "boards") of the side (tsal - "side") of the tabernacle, for the two sides (tsal - "side") westward (yam - "west").

28. And the middle bar (bar - "bar") in the midst (tehok - "midst") of the boards (kerashim - "boards") shall pass through from end to end (mi-kets el-kets - "from end to end").

29. And you shall overlay the boards (kerashim - "boards") with gold, and make their rings of gold for places (makom - "places") for the bars (bar - "bars"), and you shall overlay the bars (bar - "bars") with gold.

30. And you shall rear up the tabernacle according to the fashion thereof which was shown you in the mount.
