Exodus 6

1. But the Lord (YHWH - Lord) said to Moses (Mosheh - drawn out), "Now you shall see what I will do to Pharaoh. For with a strong hand (Chazakah - strong hand) he will send them out, and with a strong hand (Chazakah - strong hand) he will drive them out (Garah - drive out) of his land."

2. And God (Elohim - God) spoke to Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) and said to him, "I am the Lord (YHWH - Lord).

3. I appeared (Ra'ah - appeared) to Abraham (Avraham - father of many), to Isaac (Yitzhak - he will laugh), and to Jacob (Ya'akov - heel grabber) as God Almighty (El Shaddai - God Almighty), but by my name the Lord (YHWH - Lord) I did not make myself known (Yadah - make known) to them.

4. I also established (Karat - established) my covenant (Berith - covenant) with them, to give (Natan - give) them the land of Canaan (Kana'an - Canaan), the land in which they lived (Gar - lived) as sojourners (Ger - sojourners).

5. Moreover, I have heard the groaning (Na'akat - groaning) of the people of Israel (Yisrael - God contends), whom the Egyptians hold as slaves (Eved - slaves), and I have remembered my covenant (Berith - covenant).

6. Say therefore to the people of Israel (Yisrael - God contends), 'I am the Lord (YHWH - Lord), and I will bring you out (Yats'a - bring out) from under the burdens of the Egyptians, and I will deliver (Natsal - deliver) you from slavery to them, and I will redeem (Ga'al - redeem) you with an outstretched arm (Zroa - arm) and with great acts of judgment.'

7. I will take you to be my people, and I will be your God (Elohim - God), and you shall know that I am the Lord (YHWH - Lord) your God (Elohim - God), who has brought you out (Yats'a - brought out) from under the burdens of the Egyptians.

8. I will bring you into the land that I swore (Shaba - swore) to give (Natan - give) to Abraham (Avraham - father of many), to Isaac (Yitzhak - he will laugh), and to Jacob (Ya'akov - heel grabber), and I will give it to you for a possession (Achuzah - possession). I am the Lord (YHWH - Lord).'"

9. Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) spoke thus to the people of Israel (Yisrael - God contends), but they did not listen to Moses (Mosheh - drawn out), because of their broken spirit (Ruach - spirit) and harsh (Kashah - harsh) slavery.

10. So the Lord (YHWH - Lord) said to Moses (Mosheh - drawn out),

11. "Go in, tell Pharaoh king of Egypt to let the people of Israel (Yisrael - God contends) go out of his land."

12. But Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) said to the Lord (YHWH - Lord), "Behold, the people of Israel (Yisrael - God contends) have not listened (Shama - listened) to me; how then shall Pharaoh listen to me, for I am of uncircumcised lips?"

13. But the Lord (YHWH - Lord) spoke to Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) and Aaron (Aharon - exalted), and gave them a charge (Mitzvah - command) about the people of Israel (Yisrael - God contends) and about Pharaoh, king of Egypt, to bring the people of Israel (Yisrael - God contends) out of the land of Egypt.

14. These are the heads (Rosh - heads) of their fathers' houses (Beyt - houses): The sons (Banim - sons) of Reuben (Re'uven - behold a son), Israel's (Yisrael - God contends) firstborn (Bekhor - firstborn), Hanokh (Chanokh - dedicated), Pallu (Pallu - distinguished), Hezron (Chatzron - enclosed), and Carmi (Karmi - my vineyard). These are the clans (Mishpachah - clans) of Reuben (Re'uven - behold a son).

15. The sons (Banim - sons) of Simeon (Shimon - he who hears): Jemuel (Yemuel - day of God), Jamin (Yamin - right hand), Ohad (Ohad - united), Jachin (Yakin - he will establish), Zohar (Tsohar - brightness), and Shaul (Sha'ul - asked for), the son (Ben - son) of a Canaanite (Kena'an - Canaanite) woman. These are the clans (Mishpachah - clans) of Simeon (Shimon - he who hears).

16. These are the names of the sons (Banim - sons) of Levi (Levi - joined): Gershon (Gershon - exile), Kohath (Kehath - assembly), and Merari (Merari - bitter). The years of the life (Chayim - life) of Levi (Levi - joined) were 137 years.

17. The sons (Banim - sons) of Gershon (Gershon - exile): Libni (Livni - white) and Shimi (Shimi - my name). These are the clans (Mishpachah - clans) of the Gershonites (Gershomi - Gershonites).

18. The sons (Banim - sons) of Kohath (Kehath - assembly): Amram (Amram - exalted people), Izhar (Yizhar - shining), Hebron (Chevron - alliance), and Uzziel (Uzziel - my strength is God). The years of the life (Chayim - life) of Kohath (Kehath - assembly) were 133 years.

19. The sons (Banim - sons) of Merari (Merari - bitter): Mahli (Machli - sickly) and Mushi (Mushi - drawing out). These are the clans (Mishpachah - clans) of the Merarites (Merari - Merarites).

20. Amram (Amram - exalted people) took as his wife Jochebed (Yocheved - the Lord is glory), his father's sister, and she bore (Yalad - bore) him Aaron (Aharon - exalted) and Moses (Mosheh - drawn out). The years of the life (Chayim - life) of Amram (Amram - exalted people) were 137 years.

21. The sons (Banim - sons) of Izhar (Yizhar - shining): Korah (Korah - bald), Nepheg (Nefeg - blast), and Zichri (Zikhri - my memory).

22. The sons (Banim - sons) of Uzziel (Uzziel - my strength is God): Mishael (Misha'el - who is like God?), Elzaphan (Eltsaphan - God has concealed), and Zithri (Zithri - my thorn).

23. Aaron (Aharon - exalted) took as his wife Elisheba (Elisheva - God is my oath), the daughter (Bat - daughter) of Amminadab (Aminadav - my kinsman is noble), the sister of Nahshon (Nachshon - diviner). And she bore (Yalad - bore) him Nadab (Nadab - generous), Abihu (Avihu - my father is He), Eleazar (El'azar - God is my help), and Ithamar (Itamar - palm tree).

24. The sons (Banim - sons) of Korah (Korah - bald): Assir (Asir - prisoner), Elkanah (Elqanah - God has created), and Abiasaph (Aviasaf - my father has gathered). These are the clans (Mishpachah - clans) of the Korahites (Korah - Korahites).

25. Eleazar (El'azar - God is my help) the son (Ben - son) of Aaron (Aharon - exalted) took as his wife one of the daughters (Bat - daughters) of Putiel (Putiel - devoted to God), and she bore (Yalad - bore) him Phinehas (Pinchas - mouth of brass). These are the heads (Rosh - heads) of the fathers' houses (Beyt - houses) of the Levites (Levi - Levites) by their clans (Mishpachah - clans).

26. These are the Aaron (Aharon - exalted) and Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) to whom the Lord (YHWH - Lord) said, "Bring out (Yats'a - bring out) the people of Israel (Yisrael - God contends) from the land of Egypt by their hosts (Tzaba - armies)."

27. It was they who spoke (Dabar - spoke) to Pharaoh king of Egypt, about bringing out (Yats'a - bringing out) the people of Israel (Yisrael - God contends) from Egypt. It was this Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) and this Aaron (Aharon - exalted).

28. On the day when the Lord (YHWH - Lord) spoke (Dabar - spoke) to Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) in the land of Egypt,

29. the Lord (YHWH - Lord) said to Moses (Mosheh - drawn out), "I am the Lord (YHWH - Lord); tell Pharaoh king of Egypt all that I say to you."

30. But Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) said to the Lord (YHWH - Lord), "Behold, I am of uncircumcised lips, how will Pharaoh listen to me?"
