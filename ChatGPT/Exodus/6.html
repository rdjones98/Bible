Exodus 6

1. But the Lord (YHWH - He Who Exists) said to Moses (Mosheh - drawn out), "Now you shall see what I will do to Pharaoh. For with a strong hand he will send them out, and with a strong hand he will drive them out of his land."

2. And God (Elohim - Mighty Ones) spoke to Moses and said to him, "I am the Lord.

3. I appeared (Ra'ah - appeared) to Abraham (Avraham - father of many), to Isaac (Yitzhak - he will laugh), and to Jacob (Ya'akov - heel grabber) as God Almighty (El Shaddai - God Almighty), but by my name the Lord (YHWH - He who Exists) I did not make myself known to them.

4. I also established my covenant with them, to give them the land of Canaan (Kana'an - Canaan), the land in which they lived as sojourners.

5. Moreover, I have heard the groaning of the people of Israel, whom the Egyptians hold as slaves, and I have remembered my covenant.

6. Say therefore to the people of Israel, 'I am the Lord, and I will bring you out from under the burdens of the Egyptians, and I will deliver you from slavery to them, and I will redeem you with an outstretched arm and with great acts of judgment.'

7. I will take you to be my people, and I will be your God, and you shall know that I am the Lord your God, who has brought you out from under the burdens of the Egyptians.

8. I will bring you into the land that I swore to give to Abraham (Avraham - father of many), to Isaac (Yitzhak - he will laugh), and to Jacob (Ya'akov - heel grabber), and I will give it to you for a possession (Achuzah - possession). I am the Lord.'"

9. Moses spoke thus to the people of Israel, but they did not listen to Moses, because of their broken spirit (Ruach - spirit) and harsh slavery.

10. So the Lord said to Moses,

11. "Go in, tell Pharaoh king of Egypt to let the people of Israel go out of his land."

12. But Moses said to the Lord, "Behold, the people of Israel have not listened to me; how then shall Pharaoh listen to me, for I am of uncircumcised lips?"

13. But the Lord spoke to Moses and Aaron (Aharon - exalted), and gave them a charge about the people of Israel and about Pharaoh, king of Egypt, to bring the people of Israel out of the land of Egypt.

14. These are the heads of their fathers' houses: The sons of Reuben (Re'uven - behold a son), Israel's firstborn, Hanokh (Chanokh - dedicated), Pallu (Pallu - distinguished), Hezron (Chatzron - enclosed), and Carmi (Karmi - my vineyard). These are the clans (Mishpachah - clans) of Reuben (Re'uven - behold a son).

15. The sons of Simeon (Shimon - he who hears): Jemuel (Yemuel - day of God), Jamin (Yamin - right hand), Ohad (Ohad - united), Jachin (Yakin - he will establish), Zohar (Tsohar - brightness), and Shaul (Sha'ul - asked for), the son of a Canaanite (Kena'an - Canaanite) woman. These are the clans (Mishpachah - clans) of Simeon (Shimon - he who hears).

16. These are the names of the sons of Levi (Levi - joined): Gershon (Gershon - exile), Kohath (Kehath - assembly), and Merari (Merari - bitter). The years of the life (Chayim - life) of Levi (Levi - joined) were 137 years.

17. The sons of Gershon (Gershon - exile): Libni (Livni - white) and Shimi (Shimi - my name). These are the clans (Mishpachah - clans) of the Gershonites (Gershomi - Gershonites).

18. The sons of Kohath (Kehath - assembly): Amram (Amram - exalted people), Izhar (Yizhar - shining), Hebron (Chevron - alliance), and Uzziel (Uzziel - my strength is God). The years of the life (Chayim - life) of Kohath (Kehath - assembly) were 133 years.

19. The sons of Merari (Merari - bitter): Mahli (Machli - sickly) and Mushi (Mushi - drawing out). These are the clans (Mishpachah - clans) of the Merarites (Merari - Merarites).

20. Amram (Amram - exalted people) took as his wife Jochebed (Yocheved - the Lord is glory), his father's sister, and she bore him Aaron (Aharon - exalted) and Moses. The years of the life (Chayim - life) of Amram (Amram - exalted people) were 137 years.

21. The sons of Izhar (Yizhar - shining): Korah (Korah - bald), Nepheg (Nefeg - blast), and Zichri (Zikhri - my memory).

22. The sons of Uzziel (Uzziel - my strength is God): Mishael (Misha'el - who is like God?), Elzaphan (Eltsaphan - God has concealed), and Zithri (Zithri - my thorn).

23. Aaron (Aharon - exalted) took as his wife Elisheba (Elisheva - God is my oath), the daughter (Bat - daughter) of Amminadab (Aminadav - my kinsman is noble), the sister of Nahshon (Nachshon - diviner). And she bore him Nadab (Nadab - generous), Abihu (Avihu - my father is He), Eleazar (El'azar - God is my help), and Ithamar (Itamar - palm tree).

24. The sons of Korah (Korah - bald): Assir (Asir - prisoner), Elkanah (Elqanah - God has created), and Abiasaph (Aviasaf - my father has gathered). These are the clans (Mishpachah - clans) of the Korahites (Korah - Korahites).

25. Eleazar (El'azar - God is my help) the son of Aaron (Aharon - exalted) took as his wife one of the daughters of Putiel (Putiel - devoted to God), and she bore him Phinehas (Pinchas - mouth of brass). These are the heads of the fathers' houses of the Levites (Levi - Levites) by their clans (Mishpachah - clans).

26. These are the Aaron (Aharon - exalted) and Moses to whom the Lord said, "Bring out the people of Israel from the land of Egypt by their hosts."

27. It was they who spoke to Pharaoh king of Egypt, about bringing out the people of Israel from Egypt. It was this Moses and this Aaron (Aharon - exalted).

28. On the day when the Lord spoke to Moses in the land of Egypt,

29. the Lord said to Moses, "I am the Lord; tell Pharaoh king of Egypt all that I say to you."

30. But Moses said to the Lord, "Behold, I am of uncircumcised lips, how will Pharaoh listen to me?"
