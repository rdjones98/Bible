Exodus

1. And the LORD (YHWH - I AM) spoke unto Moses (Mosheh - drawn out), saying,

2. On the first day of the first month (Chodesh - month) shalt thou set up the tabernacle (Mishkan - dwelling of the congregation) of the tent (Ohel - tent) of the congregation (Mo'ed - meeting).

3. And thou shalt put therein the ark (Aron - ark) of the testimony (Edut - testimony), and cover the ark (Aron - ark) with the veil (Parokheth - screen).

4. And thou shalt bring in the table (Shulchan - table), and set in order the things that are to be set in order upon it; and thou shalt bring in the candlestick (Menorah - lampstand), and light the lamps thereof.

5. And thou shalt set the altar (Mizbeach - altar) of gold (Zahav - gold) for the incense (Katorit - incense) before the ark (Aron - ark) of the testimony (Edut - testimony), and put the hanging (Kor - curtain) of the door (Sha'ar - gate) to the tabernacle (Mishkan - dwelling).

6. And thou shalt set the altar (Mizbeach - altar) of the burnt offering (Olah - whole burnt offering) before the door (Sha'ar - gate) of the tabernacle (Mishkan - dwelling) of the tent (Ohel - tent of the congregation (Mo'ed - meeting)).

7. And thou shalt set the laver (Kior - basin) between the tent (Ohel - tent) of the congregation (Mo'ed - meeting) and the altar (Mizbeach - altar), and shalt put water (Mayim - water) therein.

8. And thou shalt set up the court (Chatzar - courtyard) round about, and hang up the hanging (Kor - curtain) at the court (Chatzar - courtyard) gate (Sha'ar - gate).

9. And thou shalt take the anointing oil (Shemen - oil), and anoint the tabernacle (Mishkan - dwelling) and all that is therein, and shalt hallow it, and all the vessels (Keilim - vessels) thereof: and it shall be holy (Kadosh - holy).

10. And thou shalt anoint the altar (Mizbeach - altar) of the burnt offering (Olah - whole burnt offering), and all his vessels (Keilim - vessels), and sanctify the altar (Mizbeach - altar): and it shall be an altar (Mizbeach - altar most holy (Kadosh - holy)).

11. And thou shalt anoint the laver (Kior - basin) and his foot (Yarekh - base), and sanctify it.

12. And thou shalt bring Aaron (Aharon - exalted) and his sons (Banim - sons) unto the door (Sha'ar - gate) of the tabernacle (Mishkan - dwelling) of the congregation (Mo'ed - meeting), and wash them with water (Mayim - water).

13. And thou shalt put upon Aaron (Aharon - exalted) the holy garments (Bigdei Hakodesh - garments of holiness), and anoint him, and sanctify him; that he may minister unto me in the priest's office (Kohanim - priesthood).

14. And thou shalt bring his sons (Banim - sons), and clothe them with coats (Ketonet - tunics):

15. And thou shalt anoint them, as thou didst anoint their father (Av - father), that they may minister unto me in the priest's office (Kohanim - priesthood): for their anointing shall surely be an everlasting priesthood (Kehunah - priesthood) throughout their generations (Dor - generations).

16. Thus did Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) according to all that the LORD (YHWH - I AM) commanded him, so did he.

17. And it came to pass in the first month (Chodesh - month) in the second year (Shanah - year), on the first day of the month (Chodesh - month), that the tabernacle (Mishkan - dwelling) was reared up.

18. And Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) reared up the tabernacle (Mishkan - dwelling), and fastened his sockets (Adan - bases), and set up the boards (Kerashim - boards) thereof, and put in the bars (Barot - bars) thereof, and reared up his pillars (Amudim - pillars),

19. And he spread abroad the tent (Ohel - tent) over the tabernacle (Mishkan - dwelling), and put the covering (Me'aseh - covering) of the tent (Ohel - tent) upon it; as the LORD (YHWH - I AM) commanded Moses (Mosheh - drawn out).

20. And he took and put the testimony (Edut - testimony) into the ark (Aron - ark), and set the staves (Amudim - poles) on the ark (Aron - ark), and put the mercy seat (Kaporet - mercy seat) above upon the ark (Aron - ark):

21. And he brought the ark (Aron - ark) into the tabernacle (Mishkan - dwelling), and set up the veil (Parokheth - screen of the covering) of the door (Sha'ar - gate), and the ark (Aron - ark) of the testimony (Edut - testimony); as the LORD (YHWH - I AM) commanded Moses (Mosheh - drawn out).

22. And he put the table (Shulchan - table in the tent of the congregation (Mo'ed - meeting), upon the side of the tabernacle (Mishkan - dwelling) northward, without the veil (Parokheth - screen).

23. And he set the bread (Lechem - bread) in order upon it before the LORD (YHWH - I AM); as the LORD (YHWH - I AM) had commanded Moses (Mosheh - drawn out).

24. And he put the candlestick (Menorah - lampstand) in the tent (Ohel - tent) of the congregation (Mo'ed - meeting), over against the table (Shulchan - table), on the side of the tabernacle (Mishkan - dwelling, southward).

25. And he lighted the lamps (Nerot - lamps) before the LORD (YHWH - I AM); as the LORD (YHWH - I AM) commanded Moses (Mosheh - drawn out).

26. And he put the golden altar (Mizbeach - altar) in the tent (Ohel - tent) of the congregation (Mo'ed - meeting) before the veil (Parokheth - screen):

27. And he burnt sweet incense (Katorit - incense) thereon; as the LORD (YHWH - I AM) commanded Moses (Mosheh - drawn out).

28. And he set up the hanging (Kor - curtain) at the door (Sha'ar - gate) of the tabernacle (Mishkan - dwelling).

29. And he put the altar (Mizbeach - altar) of the burnt offering (Olah - whole burnt offering) by the door (Sha'ar - gate) of the tabernacle (Mishkan - dwelling) of the tent (Ohel - tent of the congregation (Mo'ed - meeting)), and offered upon it the burnt offering (Olah - whole burnt offering) and the meat offering (Minchah - meal offering); as the LORD (YHWH - I AM) commanded Moses (Mosheh - drawn out).

30. And he set the laver (Kior - basin) between the tent (Ohel - tent) of the congregation (Mo'ed - meeting) and the altar (Mizbeach - altar), and put water (Mayim - water) therein, to wash withal.

31. And Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) and Aaron (Aharon - exalted) and his sons (Banim - sons) washed their hands (Yad - hands) and their feet (Raglaihem - feet) thereat:

32. When they went into the tent (Ohel - tent) of the congregation (Mo'ed - meeting), and when they came near unto the altar (Mizbeach - altar), they washed; as the LORD (YHWH - I AM) commanded Moses (Mosheh - drawn out).

33. And he reared up the court (Chatzar - courtyard) round about the tabernacle (Mishkan - dwelling) and the altar (Mizbeach - altar), and set up the hanging (Kor - curtain) of the court (Chatzar - courtyard) gate (Sha'ar - gate). So Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) finished the work (Ma'aseh - work).

34. Then a cloud (Anan - cloud) covered the tent (Ohel - tent) of the congregation (Mo'ed - meeting), and the glory (Kavod - glory) of the LORD (YHWH - I AM) filled the tabernacle (Mishkan - dwelling).

35. And Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) was not able to enter into the tent (Ohel - tent) of the congregation (Mo'ed - meeting), because the cloud (Anan - cloud) abode thereon, and the glory (Kavod - glory) of the LORD (YHWH - I AM) filled the tabernacle (Mishkan - dwelling).

36. And when the cloud (Anan - cloud) was taken up from over the tabernacle (Mishkan - dwelling), the children (Bnei - children) of Israel (Yisrael - God prevails) went onward, in all their journeys (Mas'ah - journeys):

37. But if the cloud (Anan - cloud) were not taken up, then they journeyed not till the day that it was taken up.

38. For the cloud (Anan - cloud) of the LORD (YHWH - I AM) was upon the tabernacle (Mishkan - dwelling) by day, and fire (Esh - fire) was on it by night, in the sight (Le'eynehem - sight) of all the house (Beit - house) of Israel (Yisrael - God prevails), throughout all their journeys (Mas'ah - journeys).
