Exodus 39 - Literal Translation

1. And of the blue, and purple, and scarlet, they made clothes of service, to do service in the holy place (Mikdash - holy place), and made the holy garments (Bigdei Hakodesh - garments of holiness) for Aaron (Aharon - exalted); as the LORD (YHWH - I AM) commanded Moses (Mosheh - drawn out).

2. And he made the ephod (Ephod - apron) of gold (Zahav - gold), blue, and purple, and scarlet, and fine twined linen.

3. And they did beat the gold (Zahav - gold) into thin plates, and cut it into wires, to work it in the blue, and in the purple, and in the scarlet, and in the fine linen, with cunning work.

4. They made shoulderpieces (Katayim - shoulder straps) for it, to couple it; by the two edges was it coupled together.

5. And the curious girdle (Avnet - sash) of his ephod (Ephod - apron) that was upon it, was of the same, according to the work thereof; of gold (Zahav - gold), blue, and purple, and scarlet, and fine twined linen; as the LORD (YHWH - I AM) commanded Moses (Mosheh - drawn out).

6. And they wrought onyx stones (Shoham - onyx) enclosed in ouches (Taba'ot - settings) of gold (Zahav - gold), graven, as signets are graven, with the names of the children of Israel (Yisrael - God prevails).

7. And he put them on the shoulders (Katayim - shoulder straps) of the ephod (Ephod - apron), that they should be stones of memorial for the children of Israel (Yisrael - God prevails); as the LORD (YHWH - I AM) commanded Moses (Mosheh - drawn out).

8. And he made the breastplate (Choshen - breastplate) of cunning work, like the work of the ephod (Ephod - apron); of gold (Zahav - gold), blue, and purple, and scarlet, and fine twined linen.

9. It was four square; they made the breastplate (Choshen - breastplate) double: a span was the length thereof, and a span the breadth thereof, being doubled.

10. And they set in it four rows of stones (Even - stones): the first row was a sardius (Odem - ruby), a topaz (Pitdah - chrysolite), and a carbuncle (Bareqeth - emerald): this was the first row.

11. And the second row, a turquoise (Nofech - turquoise), a sapphire (Sappir - sapphire), and a diamond (Yahalom - diamond): this was the second row.

12. And the third row, a jacinth (Leshem - jacinth), an agate (Shebo - agate), and an amethyst (Achlamah - amethyst): this was the third row.

13. And the fourth row, a beryl (Tarshish - beryl), and an onyx (Shoham - onyx), and a jasper (Yashpheh - jasper): they were enclosed in ouches (Taba'ot - settings) of gold (Zahav - gold) in their enclosings.

14. And the stones (Even - stones) were according to the names of the children of Israel (Yisrael - God prevails), twelve, according to their names, like the engravings of a signet, every one with his name, according to the twelve tribes (Shivtei Yisrael - tribes of Israel).

15. And they made upon the breastplate (Choshen - breastplate) chains (Chavlayim - chains) at the ends, of wreathen work (Zahav - gold), of pure gold (Zahav - gold).

16. And they made two gold (Zahav - gold) rings, and put them on the two ends of the breastplate (Choshen - breastplate).

17. And they put the two wreathen chains (Chavlayim - chains) of gold (Zahav - gold) in the two rings on the ends of the breastplate (Choshen - breastplate).

18. And the other two ends of the two wreathen chains (Chavlayim - chains) they fastened to the two ouches (Taba'ot - settings) and put them on the shoulders (Katayim - shoulder straps) of the ephod (Ephod - apron), before it.

19. And they made two rings of gold (Zahav - gold), and put them on the two sides of the breastplate (Choshen - breastplate), upon the border of it, which was on the side of the ephod (Ephod - apron) inward.

20. And they made two other golden rings (Taba'ot - rings), and put them on the two sides of the ephod (Ephod - apron) underneath, toward the forepart of it, over against the other coupling thereof, above the curious girdle (Avnet - sash) of the ephod (Ephod - apron).

21. And they bound the breastplate (Choshen - breastplate) by his rings (Taba'ot - rings) unto the rings of the ephod (Ephod - apron) with a lace (Chavil - cord) of blue (Techelet - blue), that it might be above the curious girdle (Avnet - sash) of the ephod (Ephod - apron), and that the breastplate (Choshen - breastplate) might not be loosed from the ephod (Ephod - apron); as the LORD (YHWH - I AM) commanded Moses (Mosheh - drawn out).

22. And he made the robe (Me'il - robe) of the ephod (Ephod - apron) of woven work (Ma'aseh - work) all of blue (Techelet - blue):

23. And there was a hole in the midst of the robe (Me'il - robe), as the hole of an habergeon, with a band round about the hole, that it should not rend.

24. And they made upon the hems of the robe (Me'il - robe) pomegranates (Rimmon - pomegranates) of blue, and purple, and scarlet, and twined linen.

25. And they made bells (Pera' - bells) of pure gold (Zahav - gold), and put the bells (Pera' - bells) between the pomegranates (Rimmon - pomegranates) upon the hem of the robe (Me'il - robe), round about between the pomegranates (Rimmon - pomegranates);

26. A bell (Pera' - bell) and a pomegranate (Rimmon - pomegranate), a bell (Pera' - bell) and a pomegranate (Rimmon - pomegranate), upon the hem of the robe (Me'il - robe) round about.

27. And they made coats (Ketonet - tunics) of fine linen (Shesh - fine linen) of woven work (Ma'aseh - work) for Aaron (Aharon - exalted) and for his sons (Banim - sons),

28. And a mitre (Mitznefet - turban) of fine linen (Shesh - fine linen), and goodly bonnets (Migba'ah - caps) of fine linen (Shesh - fine linen), and linen (Shesh - fine linen) breeches (Michnasayim - trousers) of fine twined linen (Shesh - fine linen),

29. And a girdle (Avnet - sash) of fine twined linen (Shesh - fine linen), and blue (Techelet - blue), and purple (Argaman - purple), and scarlet (Tola'at - scarlet), of needlework (Ma'aseh - work); as the LORD (YHWH - I AM) commanded Moses (Mosheh - drawn out).

30. And they made the plate (Tzitz - plate) of the holy crown (Nezer - crown) of pure gold (Zahav - gold), and wrote upon it a writing, like to the engravings of a signet, HOLINESS TO THE LORD (KODESH LA-YHWH - holiness to the LORD),

31. And they tied unto it a lace (Chavil - cord) of blue (Techelet - blue), to fasten it on high upon the mitre (Mitznefet - turban); as the LORD (YHWH - I AM) commanded Moses (Mosheh - drawn out).

32. Thus was all the work (Ma'aseh - work) of the tabernacle (Mishkan - dwelling of the congregation (Edah - assembly)) of the tent of the congregation (Ohel Mo'ed - tent of meeting) finished: and the children of Israel (Benei Yisrael - children of Israel) did according to all that the LORD (YHWH - I AM) commanded Moses (Mosheh - drawn out); so did they.

33. And they brought the tabernacle (Mishkan - dwelling) unto Moses (Mosheh - drawn out), the tent (Ohel - tent), and all his furniture (Kelei - vessels), his taches (Tach - hooks), his boards (Kerashim - boards), his bars (Barot - bars), and his pillars (Amudim - pillars), and his sockets (Adan - bases),

34. And the covering (Me'aseh - covering) of rams' skins dyed red (Tola'at - scarlet), and the covering (Me'aseh - covering) of badgers' skins (Techashim - sea cow),

35. And the veil (Parokheth - screen) of the covering (Me'aseh - covering), and the ark (Aron - ark) of the testimony (Edut - testimony), and the staves (Amudim - poles) thereof, and the mercy seat (Kaporet - mercy seat),

36. And the table (Shulchan - table) and all the vessels (Keilim - vessels) thereof, and the shewbread (Lechem - bread) of presence (Hapanim - of faces),

37. The candlestick (Menorah - lampstand), and the lamps (Nerot - lamps) thereof, even the lamps (Nerot - lamps) to be set in order, and all the vessels (Keilim - vessels) thereof, and the oil (Shemen - oil) for light (Ma'or - light),

38. And the altar (Mizbeach - altar of incense), and his grate (Mirtak - grate), and his staves (Amudim - poles), and the anointing oil (Shemen - oil), and the sweet incense (Katorit - incense), and the hanging (Kor - curtain) for the door (Sha'ar - gate) of the tabernacle (Mishkan - dwelling),

39. The brazen (Nechoshet - copper) altar (Mizbeach - altar) with his grate (Mirtak - grate of copper), his staves (Amudim - poles), and all his vessels (Keilim - vessels), the laver (Kior - basin) and his foot (Yarekh - base),

40. The hangings (Kor - curtains) of the court (Chatzar - courtyard), his pillars (Amudim - pillars), and his sockets (Adan - bases), and the hanging (Kor - curtain) for the gate (Sha'ar - gate) of the court (Chatzar - courtyard),

41. The pins (Yatad - pegs) of the tabernacle (Mishkan - dwelling), and the pins (Yatad - pegs) of the court (Chatzar - courtyard), and their cords (Chavlayim - cords),

42. According to all that the LORD (YHWH - I AM) commanded Moses (Mosheh - drawn out), so the children of Israel (Benei Yisrael - children of Israel) made all the work (Ma'aseh - work).

43. And Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) did look upon all the work (Ma'aseh - work), and, behold, they had done it as the LORD (YHWH - I AM) had commanded, even so had they done it: and Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) blessed them.
