Exodus 38 - Literal Translation

1. And he made the altar (Mizbeach - altar) of burnt offering (Olah - whole offering) of shittim wood: five cubits was the length thereof, and five cubits the breadth thereof; it was four square, and three cubits the height thereof.

2. And he made the horns (Keren - horn) of it on the four corners thereof; the horns thereof were of the same: and he overlaid it with brass (Nechoshet - copper).

3. And he made all the vessels of the altar (Mizbeach - altar), the pots (Kerah - basins), and the shovels (Machtah - firepans), and the basins (Mizrak - bowls), and the fleshhooks (Machatset - forks), and the firepans (Machtah - pans); all the vessels thereof made he of brass (Nechoshet - copper).

4. And he made for the altar (Mizbeach - altar) a brazen (Nechoshet - copper) grate of network under the compass thereof beneath unto the midst of it.

5. And he cast four rings for the four ends of the grate of brass (Nechoshet - copper), to be places for the staves (poles).

6. And he made the staves (poles) of shittim wood, and overlaid them with brass (Nechoshet - copper).

7. And he put the staves (poles) into the rings on the sides of the altar (Mizbeach - altar), to bear it withal; he made the altar (Mizbeach - altar) hollow with boards (Shittim - wood).

8. And he made the laver (Kior - basin) of brass (Nechoshet - copper), and the foot of it of brass (Nechoshet - copper), of the looking glasses (Mara'ot - mirrors) of the women assembling, which assembled at the door of the tabernacle (Mishkan - dwelling) of the congregation (Edah - assembly).

9. And he made the court (Chatzar - courtyard) on the south side southward: the hangings (Kor - curtains) of the court (Chatzar - courtyard) were of fine twined linen, an hundred cubits:

10. Their twenty pillars (Amudim - pillars) and their twenty sockets (Adan - bases) were of brass (Nechoshet - copper); the hooks (Tach - clasps) of the pillars (Amudim - pillars) and their fillets (Taba'at - connecting pieces) were of silver (Kesef - silver).

11. And for the north side the hangings (Kor - curtains) were an hundred cubits, their twenty pillars (Amudim - pillars) and their twenty sockets (Adan - bases) of brass (Nechoshet - copper); the hooks (Tach - clasps) of the pillars (Amudim - pillars) and their fillets (Taba'at - connecting pieces) of silver (Kesef - silver).

12. And for the west side were hangings (Kor - curtains) of fifty cubits: their ten pillars (Amudim - pillars) and their ten sockets (Adan - bases) of brass (Nechoshet - copper); the hooks (Tach - clasps) of the pillars (Amudim - pillars) and their fillets (Taba'at - connecting pieces) of silver (Kesef - silver).

13. And for the east side eastward fifty cubits.

14. The hangings (Kor - curtains) of one side of the gate were fifteen cubits: their three pillars (Amudim - pillars) and their three sockets (Adan - bases):

15. And on the other side the hangings (Kor - curtains) were fifteen cubits: their three pillars (Amudim - pillars) and their three sockets (Adan - bases).

16. All the hangings (Kor - curtains) of the court (Chatzar - courtyard) round about were of fine twined linen.

17. And the sockets (Adan - bases) for the pillars (Amudim - pillars) were of brass (Nechoshet - copper); the hooks (Tach - clasps) of the pillars (Amudim - pillars) and their fillets (Taba'at - connecting pieces) of silver (Kesef - silver); and the overlaying of their chapiters (Keter - capitals) of silver (Kesef - silver); and all the pillars (Amudim - pillars) of the court (Chatzar - courtyard) were filleted with silver (Kesef - silver).

18. And the hanging (Kor - curtain) for the gate (Sha'ar - gate) of the court (Chatzar - courtyard) was needlework, of blue, and purple, and scarlet, and fine twined linen: and twenty cubits was the length, and the height in the breadth was five cubits, answerable to the hangings (Kor - curtains) of the court (Chatzar - courtyard).

19. And their four pillars (Amudim - pillars) and their four sockets (Adan - bases) were of brass (Nechoshet - copper; their hooks (Tach - clasps) of silver (Kesef - silver), and the overlaying of their chapiters (Keter - capitals) and their fillets (Taba'at - connecting pieces) of silver (Kesef - silver).

20. And all the pins (Yatad - pegs) of the tabernacle (Mishkan - dwelling), and of the court (Chatzar - courtyard) round about, were of brass (Nechoshet - copper).

21. This is the sum of the tabernacle (Mishkan - dwelling), even of the tabernacle (Mishkan - dwelling) of testimony (Edut - testimony), as it was counted, according to the commandment of Moses (Mosheh - drawn out), for the service of the Levites (Levi'im - Levites), in the hand of Ithamar (Ithamar - palm of the tree), son of Aaron (Aharon - exalted) the priest (Kohen - priest);

22. And Bezaleel (Betzalel - in the shadow of God) the son of Uri (Uri - my light), the son of Hur (Chur - white), of the tribe of Judah (Yehudah - praised), made all that the LORD (YHWH - I AM) commanded Moses (Mosheh - drawn out),

23. And with him was Aholiab (Oholiav - my tent is my father), son of Ahisamach (Achisamach - my brother has supported), of the tribe of Dan (Dan - judge); an engraver, and a cunning workman, and an embroiderer in blue, and in purple, and in scarlet, and fine linen.

24. All the gold (Zahav - gold) that was occupied for the work in all the work of the holy place (Mikdash - holy place), even the gold (Zahav - gold) of the offering, was twenty and nine talents, and seven hundred and thirty shekels, after the shekel of the sanctuary (Miqdash - holy place):

25. And the silver (Kesef - silver) of them that were numbered of the congregation was an hundred talents, and a thousand seven hundred and threescore and fifteen shekels, after the shekel of the sanctuary (Miqdash - holy place):

26. A bekah for every man, that is, half a shekel, after the shekel of the sanctuary (Miqdash - holy place), for every one that went to be numbered, from twenty years old and upward, for six hundred thousand and three thousand and five hundred and fifty men.

27. And of the hundred talents of silver (Kesef - silver) were cast the sockets (Adan - bases) of the sanctuary (Mikdash - holy place), and the sockets (Adan - bases) of the veil (Parokheth - screen); an hundred sockets (Adan - bases) of the hundred talents, a talent for a socket (Adan - base).

28. And of the thousand seven hundred seventy and five shekels he made hooks (Tach - clasps) for the pillars (Amudim - pillars), and overlaid their chapiters (Keter - capitals), and made fillets (Taba'at - connecting pieces) for them.

29. And the brass (Nechoshet - copper) of the offering was seventy talents, and two thousand and four hundred shekels.

30. And therewith he made the sockets (Adan - bases) to the door of the tabernacle (Mishkan - dwelling of the congregation (Edah - assembly), and the brasen (Nechoshet - copper) altar (Mizbeach - altar) and the brasen (Nechoshet - copper) grate for it, and all the vessels of the altar (Mizbeach - altar),

31. And the sockets (Adan - bases) of the court (Chatzar - courtyard), and the sockets (Adan - bases) of the court (Chatzar - courtyard gate) of the court (Chatzar - courtyard) round about, and all the pins (Yatad - pegs) of the tabernacle (Mishkan - dwelling), and all the pins (Yatad - pegs) of the court (Chatzar - courtyard).
