Joshua (Yehoshua - YHWH is salvation) Chapter 4 - Modern English

1 When the whole nation had finished crossing the Jordan (Yarden - descender), YHWH (YHWH) said to Joshua (Yehoshua - YHWH is salvation),

2 "Choose twelve men from among the people, one from each tribe,

3 and tell them, ‘Take twelve stones from the middle of the Jordan (Yarden - descender), from right where the priests are standing, and carry them over with you and put them down at the place where you stay tonight.’”

4 So Joshua (Yehoshua - YHWH is salvation) called together the twelve men he had appointed from the Israelites (Yisrael - he struggles with God), one from each tribe,

5 and said to them, “Go over before the ark of YHWH (YHWH) your God (Elohim - God) into the middle of the Jordan (Yarden - descender). Each of you is to take up a stone on his shoulder, according to the number of the tribes of the Israelites (Yisrael - he struggles with God),

6 to serve as a sign among you. In the future, when your children ask you, ‘What do these stones mean?’

7 tell them that the flow of the Jordan (Yarden - descender) was cut off before the ark of the covenant of YHWH (YHWH). When it crossed the Jordan (Yarden - descender), the waters of the Jordan (Yarden - descender) were cut off. These stones are to be a memorial to the people of Israel (Yisrael - he struggles with God) forever.”

8 So the Israelites (Yisrael - he struggles with God) did as Joshua (Yehoshua - YHWH is salvation) commanded them. They took twelve stones from the middle of the Jordan (Yarden - descender), according to the number of the tribes of the Israelites (Yisrael - he struggles with God), as YHWH (YHWH) had told Joshua (Yehoshua - YHWH is salvation); and they carried them over with them to their camp, where they put them down.

9 Joshua (Yehoshua - YHWH is salvation) also set up twelve stones in the middle of the Jordan (Yarden - descender), at the spot where the priests who carried the ark of the covenant had stood. And they are there to this day.

10 Now the priests who carried the ark remained standing in the middle of the Jordan (Yarden - descender) until everything YHWH (YHWH) had commanded Joshua (Yehoshua - YHWH is salvation) was done by the people, just as Moses (Moshe - drawn out) had directed Joshua (Yehoshua - YHWH is salvation). The people hurried over,

11 and as soon as all of them had crossed, the ark of YHWH (YHWH) and the priests came to the other side while the people watched.

12 The men of Reuben (Re'uven - see, a son), Gad (Gad - fortune), and the half-tribe of Manasseh (Menasheh - causing to forget) crossed over, ready for battle, in front of the Israelites (Yisrael - he struggles with God), as Moses (Moshe - drawn out) had directed them.

13 About forty thousand armed for battle crossed over before YHWH (YHWH) to the plains of Jericho (Yericho - fragrance) for war.

14 That day YHWH (YHWH) exalted Joshua (Yehoshua - YHWH is salvation) in the sight of all Israel (Yisrael - he struggles with God); and they revered him all the days of his life, just as they had revered Moses (Moshe - drawn out).

15 Then YHWH (YHWH) said to Joshua (Yehoshua - YHWH is salvation),

16 “Command the priests carrying the ark of the covenant law to come up out of the Jordan (Yarden - descender).”

17 So Joshua (Yehoshua - YHWH is salvation) commanded the priests, “Come up out of the Jordan (Yarden - descender).”

18 And the priests came up out of the river carrying the ark of the covenant of YHWH (YHWH). No sooner had they set their feet on dry ground than the waters of the Jordan (Yarden - descender) returned to their place and ran at flood stage as before.

19 On the tenth day of the first month, the people went up from the Jordan (Yarden - descender) and camped at Gilgal (Gilgal - circle) on the eastern border of Jericho (Yericho - fragrance).

20 And Joshua (Yehoshua - YHWH is salvation) set up at Gilgal (Gilgal - circle) the twelve stones they had taken out of the Jordan (Yarden - descender).

21 He said to the Israelites (Yisrael - he struggles with God), “In the future when your descendants ask their parents, ‘What do these stones mean?’

22 tell them, ‘Israel (Yisrael - he struggles with God) crossed the Jordan (Yarden - descender) on dry ground.’

23 For YHWH (YHWH) your God (Elohim - God) dried up the Jordan (Yarden - descender) before you until you had crossed over. YHWH (YHWH) your God (Elohim - God) did to the Jordan (Yarden - descender) what he had done to the Red Sea (Yam Suf - Sea of Reeds) when he dried it up before us until we had crossed over.

24 He did this so that all the peoples of the earth might know that the hand of YHWH (YHWH) is powerful and so that you might always fear YHWH (YHWH) your God (Elohim - God).”
