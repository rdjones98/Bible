Psalm 3

Jesus as the fullfillment of this psalm.

A Psalm of David (David – Beloved), when he fled from Absalom (Avshalom – Father of Peace) his son.

1 LORD (YHWH – He is), how many are my foes! Many are rising up against me.

2 Many are saying of my soul, “There is no salvation for him in God (Elohim – Mighty Ones).” Selah.

3 But You, LORD, are a shield around me, my glory, and the One who lifts up my head.

4 I cried aloud to the LORD, and He answered me from His holy mountain. Selah.

5 I lay down and slept; I awoke, for the LORD sustained me.

6 I will not be afraid of ten thousands of people who have set themselves against me all around.

7 Arise, LORD! Save me, my God! For You strike all my enemies on the cheek; You break the teeth of the wicked.

8 Salvation belongs to the LORD; Your blessing be on Your people. Selah.
