Psalm 19

Jesus as the fullfillment of this psalm.

1 The heavens declare the glory of God (El – Mighty One), and the sky above proclaims the work of His hands.

2 Day to day pours out speech, and night to night reveals knowledge.

3 There is no speech, and there are no words; their voice is not heard.

4 Yet their line has gone out through all the earth, and their words to the end of the world. In them He has set a tent for the sun,

5 which comes out like a bridegroom leaving his chamber, and rejoices like a strong man to run his course.

6 Its rising is from one end of the heavens, and its circuit to the other end, and nothing is hidden from its heat.

7 The Torah of the LORD (YHWH – He is) is perfect, reviving the soul. The testimony of the LORD (YHWH – He is) is trustworthy, making the simple wise.

8 The precepts of the LORD (YHWH – He is) are right, rejoicing the heart. The commandment of the LORD (YHWH – He is) is pure, enlightening the eyes.

9 The fear of the LORD (YHWH – He is) is clean, enduring forever. The judgments of the LORD (YHWH – He is) are true; they are righteous altogether.

10 They are more desirable than gold, even much fine gold; sweeter also than honey and drippings from the honeycomb.

11 Moreover, by them Your servant is warned; in keeping them there is great reward.

12 Who can discern his errors? Cleanse me from hidden faults.

13 Keep Your servant also from arrogant sins; let them not rule over me. Then I shall be blameless and innocent of great transgression.

14 Let the words of my mouth and the meditation of my heart be acceptable in Your sight, O LORD (YHWH – He is), my rock and my redeemer.
