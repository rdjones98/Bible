Psalm 150

1. Praise the LORD (YHWH – He is)! Praise God (El – Mighty One) in His sanctuary; praise Him in the firmament of His power!

2. Praise Him for His mighty deeds; praise Him according to His abundant greatness!

3. Praise Him with the blast of the ram’s horn; praise Him with harp and lyre!

4. Praise Him with tambourine and dance; praise Him with strings and pipe!

5. Praise Him with sounding cymbals; praise Him with resounding cymbals!

6. Let everything that has breath praise the LORD (YHWH – He is)! Praise the LORD (YHWH – He is)!
