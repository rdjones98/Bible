Psalm 91

Jesus as the fullfillment of this psalm.

1 He who dwells in the secret place of the Most High (Elyon – Most High) will rest in the shadow of the Almighty (Shaddai – All-Sufficient One).

2 I will say of the LORD (YHWH – He is), “My refuge and my fortress, my God (Elohim – Mighty Ones), in whom I trust.”

3 For He will deliver you from the snare of the fowler and from the deadly pestilence.

4 He will cover you with His feathers, and under His wings you will take refuge; His truth will be your shield and armor.

5 You will not be afraid of the terror by night, nor of the arrow that flies by day,

6 nor of the pestilence that walks in darkness, nor of the destruction that lays waste at noonday.

7 A thousand may fall at your side, and ten thousand at your right hand, but it will not come near you.

8 You will only look with your eyes and see the recompense of the wicked.

9 Because you have made the LORD (YHWH – He is) your refuge, the Most High (Elyon – Most High) your dwelling place,

10 no evil will befall you, nor will any plague come near your tent.

11 For He will command His angels concerning you, to guard you in all your ways.

12 They will lift you up in their hands, so that you do not strike your foot against a stone.

13 You will tread on the lion and the cobra; the young lion and the serpent you will trample underfoot.

14 “Because he has set his love on Me, therefore I will deliver him; I will set him on high, because he knows My name.

15 He will call on Me, and I will answer him; I will be with him in trouble; I will deliver him and honor him.

16 With long life I will satisfy him, and show him My salvation.”
