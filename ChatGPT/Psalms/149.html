Jesus as the fullfillment of this psalm.

Psalm 149

1. Praise the LORD (YHWH – He is)! Sing to the LORD (YHWH – He is) a new song, His praise in the assembly of the faithful!

2. Let Israel (Yisra’el – God strives) rejoice in his Maker; let the children of Zion (Tziyon – Parched Place) be joyful in their King!

3. Let them praise His name with dancing; let them make music to Him with tambourine and lyre!

4. For the LORD (YHWH – He is) takes pleasure in His people; He adorns the humble with salvation.

5. Let the faithful exult in glory; let them shout for joy on their beds.

6. The high praises of God (Elohim – Mighty Ones) in their throats and a two-edged sword in their hands,

7. to execute vengeance on the nations and punishments on the peoples,

8. to bind their kings with chains and their nobles with iron shackles,

9. to carry out the written judgment against them. This is the honor of all His faithful ones. Praise the LORD (YHWH – He is)!
