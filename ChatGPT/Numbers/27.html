Numbers 27 - Literal Translation

1 And the daughters of Zelophehad (shadow of fear), son of Hepher (shame), son of Gilead (rocky region), son of Machir (sold), son of Manasseh (causing to forget), of the families of Manasseh (causing to forget), son of Joseph (He adds), came near. And these are the names of his daughters: Mahlah (sickness), Noah (motion), Hoglah (partridge), Milcah (queen), and Tirzah (pleasing).

2 And they stood before Moses (drawn out) and before Eleazar (God has helped) the priest, and before the leaders and all the congregation, at the entrance of the Tent of Meeting, saying,

3 "Our father died in the wilderness, and he was not in the midst of the congregation that gathered against the LORD (YHWH – He is) in the assembly of Korah (baldness), but he died in his own sin, and he had no sons."

4 "Why should the name of our father be removed from the midst of his family because he had no son? Give us a possession among the brothers of our father."

5 And Moses brought their case before the LORD (YHWH – He is).

6 And the LORD (YHWH – He is) spoke to Moses, saying,

7 "Rightly the daughters of Zelophehad (shadow of fear) speak. You shall surely give them a possession of inheritance among the brothers of their father, and you shall cause the inheritance of their father to pass to them."

8 "And to the children of Israel (God contends) you shall speak, saying: ‘If a man dies and has no son, then you shall cause his inheritance to pass to his daughter.’"

9 "And if he has no daughter, then you shall give his inheritance to his brothers."

10 "And if he has no brothers, then you shall give his inheritance to his father’s brothers."

11 "And if his father has no brothers, then you shall give his inheritance to his closest relative in his family, and he shall possess it. And it shall be for the children of Israel (God contends) a statute of judgment, as the LORD (YHWH – He is) commanded Moses."

12 And the LORD (YHWH – He is) said to Moses, "Go up to this mountain of Abarim (regions beyond), and see the land that I have given to the children of Israel (God contends)."

13 "And when you have seen it, you also shall be gathered to your people, as Aaron (light-bringer) your brother was gathered."

14 "Because you rebelled against My mouth in the wilderness of Zen (flat) in the strife of the congregation, to sanctify Me at the waters before their eyes. These are the waters of Meribah (quarreling) in Kadesh (holy), in the wilderness of Zen (flat)."

15 And Moses spoke to the LORD (YHWH – He is), saying,

16 "Let the LORD (YHWH – He is), the God of the spirits of all flesh, appoint a man over the congregation,"

17 "Who shall go out before them and who shall come in before them, and who shall lead them out and bring them in, so that the congregation of the LORD (YHWH – He is) shall not be like sheep that have no shepherd."

18 And the LORD (YHWH – He is) said to Moses, "Take for yourself Joshua (YHWH is salvation), son of Nun (continuance), a man in whom is the spirit, and lay your hand upon him."

19 "And stand him before Eleazar (God has helped) the priest and before all the congregation, and command him before their eyes."

20 "And you shall place some of your honor upon him, so that all the congregation of the children of Israel (God contends) shall listen to him."

21 "And he shall stand before Eleazar (God has helped) the priest, who shall inquire for him by the judgment of the Urim before the LORD (YHWH – He is). At his word, they shall go out, and at his word, they shall come in, he and all the children of Israel (God contends) with him, and all the congregation."

22 And Moses did as the LORD (YHWH – He is) commanded him. And he took Joshua (YHWH is salvation) and stood him before Eleazar (God has helped) the priest and before all the congregation.

23 And he laid his hands upon him and commanded him, as the LORD (YHWH – He is) spoke by the hand of Moses.
