Numbers 20 - Literal Translation

1 And the children of Israel (God contends) came, all the congregation, to the wilderness of Sin (thorn, clay) in the first month, and the people dwelled in Kadesh (holy). And Miriam (bitter) died there, and she was buried there.

2 And there was no water for the congregation, and they gathered against Moses (drawn out) and against Aaron (light-bringer).

3 And the people quarreled with Moses and spoke, saying, "Oh, that we had perished when our brothers perished before the Lord (YHWH - He Who Exists)!"

4 "And why have you brought the assembly of the Lord (YHWH - He Who Exists) to this wilderness, to die there, we and our livestock?"

5 "And why have you brought us up from Egypt (narrow place), to bring us to this evil place? Not a place of seed, or fig, or vine, or pomegranate, and there is no water to drink!"

6 And Moses and Aaron came from before the assembly to the entrance of the Tent of Meeting, and they fell on their faces, and the glory of the Lord (YHWH - He Who Exists) appeared to them.

7 And the Lord (YHWH - He Who Exists) spoke to Moses, saying,

8 "Take the rod, and assemble the congregation, you and Aaron your brother, and speak to the rock before their eyes, and it shall give its water. And you shall bring out for them water from the rock, and give drink to the congregation and their livestock."

9 And Moses took the rod from before the Lord (YHWH - He Who Exists) as He commanded him.

10 And Moses and Aaron gathered the assembly before the rock, and he said to them, "Hear now, you rebels, shall we bring out water for you from this rock?"

11 And Moses lifted his hand and struck the rock with his rod twice, and much water came out, and the congregation drank, and their livestock.

12 And the Lord (YHWH - He Who Exists) said to Moses and to Aaron, "Because you did not believe in Me, to sanctify Me before the eyes of the children of Israel, therefore you shall not bring this assembly into the land that I have given them."

13 These are the waters of Meribah (quarreling), because the children of Israel quarreled with the Lord (YHWH - He Who Exists), and He was sanctified through them.

14 And Moses sent messengers from Kadesh (holy) to the king of Edom (red), "Thus says your brother Israel: You know all the hardship that has found us."

15 "And our fathers went down to Egypt (narrow place), and we dwelled in Egypt (narrow place) many days, and the Egyptians did evil to us and to our fathers."

16 "And we cried out to the Lord (YHWH - He Who Exists), and He heard our voice, and sent an angel, and brought us out from Egypt (narrow place), and now we are in Kadesh (holy), a city on the edge of your border."

17 "Let us please pass through your land; we shall not pass through field or vineyard, and we shall not drink well water. On the kingâ€™s road we shall go; we shall not turn right or left until we pass through your border."

18 But Edom (red) said to him, "You shall not pass through me, lest I come out against you with the sword."

19 And the children of Israel said to him, "On the highway we shall go up, and if we drink your water, I and my livestock, then I shall give its price. Only let me pass on foot; nothing more."

20 But he said, "You shall not pass." And Edom (red) came out against him with a heavy people and a strong hand.

21 And Edom (red) refused to allow Israel to pass through his border, and Israel turned away from him.

22 And they journeyed from Kadesh (holy), and the children of Israel, all the congregation, came to Mount Hor (mountain of the mountain).

23 And the Lord (YHWH - He Who Exists) spoke to Moses and Aaron at Mount Hor (mountain of the mountain) on the border of the land of Edom (red), saying,

24 "Let Aaron be gathered to his people, for he shall not enter the land that I have given to the children of Israel, because you rebelled against My mouth at the waters of Meribah (quarreling)."

25 "Take Aaron and Eleazar (God has helped) his son, and bring them up to Mount Hor (mountain of the mountain)."

26 "And strip Aaron of his garments, and clothe Eleazar (God has helped) his son with them, and Aaron shall be gathered and die there."

27 And Moses did as the Lord (YHWH - He Who Exists) commanded, and they went up to Mount Hor (mountain of the mountain) before the eyes of all the congregation.

28 And Moses stripped Aaron of his garments and clothed Eleazar (God has helped) his son with them, and Aaron died there on the top of the mountain. And Moses and Eleazar (God has helped) came down from the mountain.

29 And all the congregation saw that Aaron had died, and they wept for Aaron thirty days, all the house of Israel (God contends).
