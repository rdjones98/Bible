Exodus 32 - Literal Translation

1. And when the people saw that Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) delayed to come down out of the mount, the people gathered themselves together unto Aaron (Aharon - exalted), and said unto him, Up, make us gods, which shall go before us; for as for this Moses (Mosheh - drawn out), the man that brought us up out of the land of Egypt (Mitzrayim - narrow place), we wot not what is become of him.

2. And Aaron (Aharon - exalted) said unto them, Break off the golden earrings, which are in the ears of your wives, of your sons, and of your daughters, and bring them unto me.

3. And all the people brake off the golden earrings which were in their ears, and brought them unto Aaron (Aharon - exalted).

4. And he received them at their hand, and fashioned it with a graving tool, after he had made it a molten calf: and they said, These be thy gods, O Israel (Yisrael - He who struggles with God), which brought thee up out of the land of Egypt (Mitzrayim - narrow place).

5. And when Aaron (Aharon - exalted) saw it, he built an altar before it; and Aaron (Aharon - exalted) made proclamation, and said, Tomorrow is a feast to the LORD (YHWH - I AM).

6. And they rose up early on the morrow, and offered burnt offerings, and brought peace offerings; and the people sat down to eat and to drink, and rose up to play.

7. And the LORD (YHWH - I AM) said unto Moses (Mosheh - drawn out), Go, get thee down; for thy people, which thou broughtest out of the land of Egypt (Mitzrayim - narrow place), have corrupted themselves:

8. They have turned aside quickly out of the way which I commanded them: they have made them a molten calf, and have worshipped it, and have sacrificed thereunto, and said, These be thy gods, O Israel (Yisrael - He who struggles with God), which have brought thee up out of the land of Egypt (Mitzrayim - narrow place).

9. And the LORD (YHWH - I AM) said unto Moses (Mosheh - drawn out), I have seen this people, and, behold, it is a stiffnecked people:

10. Now therefore let me alone, that my wrath may wax hot against them, and that I may consume them: and I will make of thee a great nation.

11. And Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) besought the LORD (YHWH - I AM) his God (Elohim - God), and said, LORD (YHWH - I AM), why doth thy wrath wax hot against thy people, which thou hast brought forth out of the land of Egypt (Mitzrayim - narrow place) with great power, and with a mighty hand?

12. Wherefore should the Egyptians (Mitzrayim - narrow place) speak, and say, For mischief did he bring them out, to slay them in the mountains, and to consume them from the face of the earth? Turn from thy fierce wrath, and repent of this evil against thy people.

13. Remember Abraham (Avraham - father of many), Isaac (Yitzchak - laughter), and Israel (Yisrael - He who struggles with God), thy servants, to whom thou swarest by thine own self, and saidst unto them, I will multiply your seed as the stars of heaven, and all this land that I have spoken of will I give unto your seed, and they shall inherit it forever.

14. And the LORD (YHWH - I AM) repented of the evil which he thought to do unto his people.

15. And Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) turned, and went down from the mount, and the two tables of the testimony were in his hand: the tables were written on both their sides; on the one side and on that side were they written.

16. And the tables were the work of God (Elohim - God), and the writing was the writing of God (Elohim - God), graven upon the tables.

17. And when Joshua (Yehoshua - salvation) heard the noise of the people as they shouted, he said unto Moses (Mosheh - drawn out), There is a noise of war in the camp.

18. And he said, It is not the voice of them that shout for mastery, neither is it the voice of them that cry for being overcome: but the noise of them that sing do I hear.

19. And it came to pass, as soon as he came nigh unto the camp, that he saw the calf, and the dancing: and Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) anger waxed hot, and he cast the tables out of his hands, and brake them beneath the mount.

20. And he took the calf which they had made, and burnt it in the fire, and ground it to powder, and strawed it upon the water, and made the children of Israel (Yisrael - He who struggles with God) drink of it.

21. And Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) said unto Aaron (Aharon - exalted), What did this people unto thee, that thou hast brought so great a sin upon them?

22. And Aaron (Aharon - exalted) said, Let not the anger of my lord wax hot: thou knowest the people, that they are set on mischief.

23. For they said unto me, Make us gods, which shall go before us: for as for this Moses (Mosheh - drawn out), the man that brought us up out of the land of Egypt (Mitzrayim - narrow place), we wot not what is become of him.

24. And I said unto them, Whosoever hath any gold, let them break it off. So they gave it me: then I cast it into the fire, and there came out this calf.

25. And when Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) saw that the people were naked; (for Aaron (Aharon - exalted) had made them naked unto their shame among their enemies),

26. Then Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) stood in the gate of the camp, and said, Who is on the LORD's (YHWH - I AM) side? let him come unto me. And all the sons of Levi (Levi - joined) gathered themselves together unto him.

27. And he said unto them, Thus saith the LORD (YHWH - I AM) God (Elohim - God) of Israel (Yisrael - He who struggles with God), Put every man his sword by his side, and go in and out from gate to gate throughout the camp, and slay every man his brother, and every man his companion, and every man his neighbour.

28. And the children of Levi (Levi - joined) did according to the word of Moses (Mosheh - drawn out): and there fell of the people that day about three thousand men.

29. For Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) had said, Consecrate yourselves today to the LORD (YHWH - I AM), even every man upon his son, and upon his brother; that he may bestow upon you a blessing this day.

30. And it came to pass on the morrow, that Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) said unto the people, Ye have sinned a great sin: and now I will go up unto the LORD (YHWH - I AM); peradventure I shall make an atonement for your sin.

31. And Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) returned unto the LORD (YHWH - I AM), and said, Oh, this people have sinned a great sin, and have made them gods of gold.

32. Yet now, if thou wilt forgive their sin, well: and if not, blot me, I pray thee, out of thy book which thou hast written.

33. And the LORD (YHWH - I AM) said unto Moses (Mosheh - drawn out), Whosoever hath sinned against me, him will I blot out of my book.

34. Therefore now go, lead the people unto the place of which I have spoken unto thee: behold, mine angel shall go before thee: nevertheless in the day when I visit I will visit their sin upon them.

35. And the LORD (YHWH - I AM) plagued the people, because they made the calf, which Aaron (Aharon - exalted) made.
