Exodus 9

1. Then the Lord (YHWH - Lord) said to Moses (Mosheh - drawn out), "Go (Bo - go) to Pharaoh (Par'oh - Pharaoh) and say (Amar - say) to him, 'Thus says (Amar - says) the Lord (YHWH - Lord), the God (Elohim - God) of the Hebrews (Ivri - Hebrews), "Let my people go (Shalach - send), that they may serve (Avad - serve) me."'

2. For if you refuse (Mane'a - refuse) to let them go (Shalach - send), and still hold them (Chazak - hold) captive,

3. behold, the hand (Yad - hand) of the Lord (YHWH - Lord) will fall (Naphal - fall) with a very severe (Chazak - severe) plague (Negah - plague) upon your livestock (Miqneh - livestock) that are in the field (Sadeh - field), on the horses (Sus - horses), on the donkeys (Chamor - donkeys), on the camels (Gamal - camels), on the oxen (Shor - oxen), and on the sheep (Tzon - sheep), a very severe (Chazak - severe) plague (Negah - plague).


Hathor - Goddess of Motherhood (cow).Apis - God of Fertility (bull). God kills all the livestock.

4. But the Lord (YHWH - Lord) will make a distinction (Pala - distinguish) between the livestock (Miqneh - livestock) of Israel (Yisra'el - Israel) and the livestock (Miqneh - livestock) of Egypt (Mitzrayim - Egypt), and nothing of all that belongs to the people of Israel (Yisra'el - Israel) shall die (Muwt - die)."

5. And the Lord (YHWH - Lord) set (Natan - set) a time (Mo'ed - time), saying (Amar - saying), "Tomorrow (Machar - tomorrow) the Lord (YHWH - Lord) will do (Asah - do) this thing (Dabar - thing) in the land (Eretz - land)."

6. And the Lord (YHWH - Lord) did this thing (Dabar - thing) on the next day (Machar - tomorrow), and all the livestock (Miqneh - livestock) of Egypt (Mitzrayim - Egypt) died (Muwt - died), but of the livestock (Miqneh - livestock) of the people of Israel (Yisra'el - Israel) not one died (Muwt - died).

7. And Pharaoh (Par'oh - Pharaoh) sent (Shalach - sent), and behold, not one of the livestock (Miqneh - livestock) of Israel (Yisra'el - Israel) was dead (Muwt - dead). But the heart (Lev - heart) of Pharaoh (Par'oh - Pharaoh) was hardened (Chazak - hardened), and he did not let the people go (Shalach - send).

8. And the Lord (YHWH - Lord) said to Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) and to Aaron (Aharon - exalted), "Take (Lakah - take) handfuls (Kaph - handful) of soot (Khamin - soot) from the kiln (Tannur - kiln) and let Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) throw (Yarash - throw) it toward the sky (Shamayim - heaven) in the sight (Chazon - sight) of Pharaoh (Par'oh - Pharaoh).

9. It shall become fine dust (Aphar - dust) over the land (Eretz - land) of Egypt (Mitzrayim - Egypt), and it shall become boils (Shehin - boils) breaking out (Parach - break out) with sores (Sheh - sores) on man (Ish - man) and beast (Behemah - beast) throughout all the land (Eretz - land) of Egypt (Mitzrayim - Egypt)."


Sekhmet - Goddess of Healing and Protection (lioness). She is unable to heal her people.

10. So they took (Lakah - took) soot (Khamin - soot) from the kiln (Tannur - kiln) and stood (Amad - stood) before Pharaoh (Par'oh - Pharaoh), and Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) threw (Yarash - threw) it toward the sky (Shamayim - heaven), and it became boils (Shehin - boils) breaking out (Parach - break out) with sores (Sheh - sores) on man (Ish - man) and beast (Behemah - beast).

11. And the magicians (Lechash - magicians) could not stand (Amad - stand) before Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) because of the boils (Shehin - boils), for the boils (Shehin - boils) came upon the magicians (Lechash - magicians) and upon all the Egyptians (Mitzrayim - Egypt).

12. But the Lord (YHWH - Lord) hardened (Chazak - hardened) the heart (Lev - heart) of Pharaoh (Par'oh - Pharaoh), and he did not listen (Shama - listen) to them, as the Lord (YHWH - Lord) had spoken (Dabar - spoken) to Moses (Mosheh - drawn out).

13. Then the Lord (YHWH - Lord) said to Moses (Mosheh - drawn out), "Rise up early and stand (Amad - stand) before Pharaoh (Par'oh - Pharaoh) and say (Amar - say) to him, 'Thus says (Amar - says) the Lord (YHWH - Lord), the God (Elohim - God) of the Hebrews (Ivri - Hebrews), "Let my people go (Shalach - send), that they may serve (Avad - serve) me."'

14. For this time (Ettah - time) I will send (Shalach - send) all my plagues (Negah - plagues) on you yourself (Atah - yourself), and on your servants and your people, so that you may know (Yadah - know) that there is none like (Kamah - like) me in all the earth (Eretz - earth).

15. For by now (Atah - now) I could have put forth (Shalach - put forth) my hand (Yad - hand) and struck (Naga - struck) you and your people with pestilence (Dever - pestilence), and you would have been cut off (Karath - cut off) from the earth (Eretz - earth).

16. But for this purpose (Avur - for this purpose) I have raised you up (Higash - raised), to show (Ra'ah - show) you my power (Koach - power), so that my name (Shem - name) may be proclaimed (Saphar - proclaimed) in all the earth (Eretz - earth).

17. You are still exalting (Ramah - exalting) yourself against (Al - against) my people, and will not let them go (Shalach - send).

18. Behold, about this time (Ettah - time) tomorrow (Machar - tomorrow), I will cause very heavy (Chazak - heavy) hail (Barad - hail) to fall (Naphal - fall) such as has never been in Egypt (Mitzrayim - Egypt) from the day (Yom - day) it was founded (Yasad - founded) until now (Atah - now).

19. Now therefore send (Shalach - send) and get (Lakah - get) your livestock (Miqneh - livestock) and all that you have (Asher - that) in the field (Sadeh - field). For every man (Ish - man) and beast (Behemah - beast) that is in the field (Sadeh - field) and is not brought (Bo - brought) home will die (Muwt - die) when the hail (Barad - hail) falls (Naphal - falls) upon them.'"


Nut - Goddess of the Sky. She is unable to control the weather.

20. Then whoever feared (Yareh - feared) the word (Dabar - word) of the Lord (YHWH - Lord) among the servants of Pharaoh (Par'oh - Pharaoh) hurried (Chush - hurried) his servants and his livestock (Miqneh - livestock) into the houses (Beyt - houses).

21. But whoever did not pay attention (Maqab - listen) to the word (Dabar - word) of the Lord (YHWH - Lord) left (Hazaq - left) his servants and his livestock (Miqneh - livestock) in the field (Sadeh - field).

22. And the Lord (YHWH - Lord) said to Moses (Mosheh - drawn out), "Stretch out (Natah - stretch out) your hand (Yad - hand) toward the sky (Shamayim - heaven), so that there may be hail (Barad - hail) in all the land (Eretz - land) of Egypt (Mitzrayim - Egypt), on man (Ish - man), and on beast (Behemah - beast), and on every plant (Esheb - plant) of the field (Sadeh - field), throughout the land (Eretz - land) of Egypt (Mitzrayim - Egypt)."

23. Then Moses (Mosheh - drawn out) stretched out (Natah - stretched out) his staff (Mateh - staff) toward the sky (Shamayim - heaven), and the Lord (YHWH - Lord) sent (Shalach - sent) thunder (Qol - thunder) and hail (Barad - hail), and fire (Esh - fire) ran down (Yarad - ran down) to the ground (Adamah - ground), and the Lord (YHWH - Lord) rained (Matar - rained) hail (Barad - hail) upon the land (Eretz - land) of Egypt (Mitzrayim - Egypt).

24. There was hail (Barad - hail) and fire (Esh - fire) flashing continually (Halak - moving), such heavy hail (Barad - hail) has never been in all the land (Eretz - land) of Egypt (Mitzrayim - Egypt) since it became a nation (Goy - nation).
